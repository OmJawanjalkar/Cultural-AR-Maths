📱 How to Use

1

Print this page at 100% scale (do not scale to fit). The marker should be at least 8 cm × 8 cm.

2

Open Cultural AR Maths and navigate to Modules → 3D Geometry Playground.

3

Allow camera access when prompted.

4

Point your camera at the black-and-white marker above. A 3D shape will appear on it.

5

Use the toolbar at the bottom to spawn shapes, slice them, unfold nets, and compare volumes.

💡 Tips for Best AR Experience

  • Keep the marker flat on a desk or table
  • Ensure good, even lighting — avoid direct glare on the marker
  • Hold the phone 20–50 cm above the marker
  • Keep the entire marker visible in frame
  • If the 3D shape disappears, slowly move the camera back toward the marker
  • No printer? Use this page on a second device's screen (works too!)
